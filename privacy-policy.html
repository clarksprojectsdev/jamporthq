Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JamPort ("we," "our," or "us"). JamPort is a music marketplace platform that connects music producers, artists, and fans. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using JamPort, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, profile information (bio, avatar, social media links), account type (artist or fan), phone number (optional, for payment processing)
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (processed securely through PayScrow), transaction history, wallet balance and transaction records. We do not store credit card numbers or full payment credentials on our servers.
  • Content You Create: Music beats you upload (audio files, metadata, artwork), event listings, in-app messages, reviews, comments, ratings, favorites and playlists.
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team, support ticket history.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, IP address.
  • Usage Information: App features you access, beats you play/like/purchase, events you view/attend, search queries, time spent in app, navigation patterns.
  • Location Information (with your permission, if enabled): Approximate location for event discovery only. We do not collect or share your precise location with other users.
  • Audio/Media Access (with your permission): Audio files you upload, images for profiles and events.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Services: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture. We do not access your Google account beyond what is necessary for authentication.
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations from PayScrow, payment status updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Create and manage accounts, process payments, deliver purchased beats/licenses, facilitate event ticket purchases, enable messaging features, provide customer support.
  • Communication: Send transaction confirmations, deliver event notifications, respond to support requests, send important service updates (you can opt out of marketing communications).
  • Analytics and Improvement: Analyze app usage, monitor performance, understand user preferences, conduct research and development.
  • Security and Compliance: Detect and prevent fraud, enforce Terms of Service, comply with legal obligations, protect user safety.

4. User-Generated Content and Moderation

JamPort provides user controls to help you manage your experience, including the ability to block other users and report inappropriate content or behavior. While we do not actively monitor all communications in real-time, we may review reported content to enforce our policies and Terms of Service. We reserve the right to remove content that violates our policies and to take appropriate action, including account suspension or termination.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

We implement industry-standard encryption, secure authentication via Firebase Auth, regular security audits, access controls, and secure payment processing through PayScrow. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, Storage, Analytics, Cloud Messaging. Privacy Policy
  • PayScrow (SafePay): Payment processing and transaction management. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Error tracking and performance monitoring. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Social login option. Privacy Policy

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., public profile information)
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (contractually obligated to protect your information)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation; to protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: Access your personal data through account settings, update or correct profile information, request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data by contacting support (some information may be retained for legal purposes)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out Options: Disable push notifications, opt out of marketing emails, disable location services, limit ad tracking
  • Account Controls: Control profile visibility, manage who can contact you, control data sharing preferences, block users, and report inappropriate content

9. Children's Privacy

JamPort is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts or use our services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly and terminate the account.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using JamPort, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app, updating the "Last Updated" date, and sending you an email notification (for material changes). Your continued use of JamPort after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Delete Account and Data

Users may request deletion of their JamPort account and associated personal data at any time. To request deletion, please send an email to jamporthq@yahoo.com with the subject line 'Delete My JamPort Account'.

Upon verification, we will permanently delete:

  • User account and profile information
  • Authentication credentials
  • User-generated content linked to the account (beats, events, uploads)

Some information may be retained for up to 30 days for legal, security, or backup purposes, after which it will be permanently removed.
